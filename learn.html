📚 PetCare Learning Center

Tips to keep your pet happy and healthy!

🐕 Grooming Tips

  • Brush fur daily to prevent matting
  • Use pet-friendly shampoo
  • Trim nails regularly

🍽 Diet Advice

  • Feed high-quality pet food
  • Fresh water available always
  • Avoid chocolate, grapes, and onions!

🏥 Emergency Care

  • Keep vet contact ready
  • Have a pet first-aid kit
  • Know signs of heatstroke and poisoning
